Histories

A tapestry of stories

Tracing the many woven narratives that makes up the vibrant history of the Ultimo area

Included in this website are the histories of the Indigneous and working people of the Ultimo area. The area has strong historical links to Indigenous Australians, whihc is represented in the 'Indigenous' history page. The strong working history of the Ultimo area, as a reuslt of its industrial past, is explored in the 'Workers'' history page. Last, the political hisotry of the area is explored in the 'Political' history page.


A timeline of a select number of events, people, and buidlings critical to Ultimo's history is also included.
