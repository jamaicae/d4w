Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Why #ULTIMOte History?

    2. The website is called #ULTIMOTe history as a play on words

  2. Who runs the website?

    3. The website is run by the Ultimo Historians who are in charge of the website's content as well as the general information maintenance of the website. Ombré, the organisation responsible for the website creation, were simply contracted to design and establish the website and no standing contract remain between The Ultimo Historians and Ombré as an organisation.

  3. Where is the information about volunteering with Ultimo Historians?

    4. Parties interested in volunteering may contact the Ultimo Historians for further information. Little experience is needed for a volunteer position other than a personable disposition and a passion for local history! The Ultimo Historians is not-for-profit organisation that depends on the efforts of its loyal and devoted volunteer staff. Contact the organisation for more information or to apply!
