About Us

#Ultimote History is a website and research resource developed by Ombré, a team of students from the University of Technology Sydney. The website was created for the Ultimo Historians

The #Ultimote history administrators and Ultimo Historians do not represent the Pyrmont Ultimo area or Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander groups in Sydney. The function of this website is largely that of an education resource and project.


Ultimo Historians

The Ultimo Historians are a not-for-profit organisation, run by a group of devoted local historians and a group of lovely volunteers. The Ultimo Historians aim to catalogue and record the vibrant history of the Pyrmont-Ultimo area. The Ultimo Historians have been functioning for 25 years as a local history community club, however, they commissioned Ombré to make them a website in order to reach a larger audience in today's modern world. Consequently, #ULTIMOTE History has been created as website for posterity and to bring the work of the Ultimo Historians to a larger audience. The Ultimo Historians hope that the website will promote the local history to others and encourage people to begin valuing their own local history.

For any questions about the history of the area, the website, or volunteering, do not hesitate to Contact Us.
