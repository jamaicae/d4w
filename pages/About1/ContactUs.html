Contact Us

The Ultimo Historians are regularly available during business hours (Mon-Fri). A small team of volunteers man the phone desks during the week, and sometimes on the weekends (this is subject to volunteer availability). Please be patient with phone calls as the Ultimo Historians is a not-for-profit organisation dependent on volunteers.

If you are an academic, educational organisation, or a similarly affiliated individual or organisation, emailing is the preffered option. The email included to the right is checked daily.

If you are looking to volunteer with #ULTIMOTe History and the Ultimo Historians complete the form below.

Please note that the Ultimo Historians will not be available for contact during public holidays or after business hours, thank you.

Ombré

Ombré is a team comprised of students from the University of Technology Sydney that were commissioned to create a website for the Ultimo Historians. They worked closely with the Ultimo Historians in order to ensure that the final website fulfilled the desire of the Ultimo Historians to record and present the history of the area for posterity. Ombré also endeavoured to provide a website that is more intuitive, accessible, and graphically inclinded than most local history websites.
